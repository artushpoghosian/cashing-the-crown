# Cashing the Crown

In **Cashing the Crown**, you are a fallen royal in a minimalist world where status is worthless without coin. Navigate abstract floating platforms and dark mazes to collect 15 golden coins—the price of the princess’s freedom. Master precision jumps to pay the ransom in full.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay
* **The Goal:** Collect exactly **15/15 coins** to unlock the final rescue.
* **Precision Platforming:** Navigate narrow branching paths and floating islands.
* **The Vault:** Survive a dark, top-down maze to find hidden treasures.
* **High Stakes:** Red platforms and gaps require perfect timing and movement.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **Engine:** Unity
* **Language:** C#
* **Art Style:** Minimalist Low-Poly

## 🚀 How to Play
1. **Movement:** Use `WASD` or `Arrow Keys` to move your character.
2. **Jump:** Press `Space` to leap across platforms.
3. **Objective:** Follow the white paths, gather all 15 coins, and reach the princess at the end of the trail.
